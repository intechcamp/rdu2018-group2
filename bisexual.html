Bisexual LGBTQ+ Community

Bisexual means that a man or a female has sexual attractions for both genders. Bisexuality is romantic attraction, sexual attraction or sexual behavior toward both males and females, or romantic or sexual attraction to people of any sex or gender identity; this later aspect is sometimes termed as pansexuality
